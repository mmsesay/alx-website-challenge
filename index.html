Each one! Teach one!

Hi, let me give is a tip about software engineering and how I will leverage it to impact my community. Software engineering is the study of and practice of engineering to build, design, develop, maintain, and retire software. There are different areas of software engineering and it serves many functions throughout the application lifecycle.

Sierra Leone

My nation is very small but there are a lot of opportunities here. Because, we are somehow tapping into technology. There comes the need are so many areas that we should automate. Areas like the health cares, births and death registrations to name a few.

The developers community is also small. It will be a heavy job for the smaller sets to work on so many projects. Therefore, passing the knowledge to fellow country men will do a lot of good and it is a contribution to the human capital development of the nation. These skilled fellow country men will join in to automate these systems with the innovative skills which will in turn fast track the development and create the smooth runnings of my nation.

Why software engineering!

Software engineering is important because specific software is needed in almost every industry, in every business, and for every function. It becomes more important as time goes on.
